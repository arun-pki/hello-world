Chemical Properties

Chemical property prediction is an important feature that ChemOffice applications support. It enables you to calculate values for a wide range of methodologies; for example, topological descriptors such as the Weiner index and Balaban index, thermodynamic descriptors such as solubility, melting and boiling points, and partition coefficients such as CLogP, properties related to the three-dimensional shape and volume of a chemical such as the Connolly surface area and volume. In total you can calculate more than 100 chemical properties for a given structure.
For a list of properties available in any specific applications, click the respective link below. For more information on the properties available in any specific applications, see the respective online help available or visit the PerkinElmer Informatics Web site.
