nLogging in to ChemDraw Cloud

 
To log in to ChemDraw Cloud:
Click File >ChemDraw Cloud, select Browse... or Upload.... If you have not yet logged in, the Login page appears.
Enter your Username and Password, and then click Sign In.
You can also access ChemDraw Cloud directly using the URL: https://chemdraw.perkinelmer.cloud.
Managing your ChemDraw Cloud Login Credentials
After you log into your ChemDraw Cloud account, you can configure ChemDraw Cloud to remember your login credentials, if desired, using the "Would you like to remember the password for the user" pop-up message that appears on the upper-right corner of the ChemDraw Cloud window.

Do one of the following:
Click Yes, to save your login credentials. When you click Yes, your login credentials will be saved and next time when you log into ChemDraw Cloud, your login credentials will be displayed automatically in the login window. After you save your credentials, you can do one of the following anytime by clicking the key icon anytime to show or hide the pop-up message box:
Click Forget It, to forget the saved credentials.
Click Keep it Remembered, to keep remembering your saved credentials.
Click Not Now, if you do not want to save your login credentials now, but on a later time.
If you click Not Now, your login credentials will not be saved, and you will get this pop-up message when you log into ChemDraw Cloud next time.
Click Never, if you do not want to save your ChemDraw Cloud login credentials.
If you click Never, your login credentials will not be saved, and you will not get this pop-up message when you log into ChemDraw Cloud next time.
