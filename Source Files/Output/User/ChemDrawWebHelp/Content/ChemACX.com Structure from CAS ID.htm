ChemACX.com Structure from CAS Number Add-in

The ChemACX.com Structure from CAS Number add-in searches the ChemACX database, retrieves the structure corresponding to the CAS number you provide and displays it in the center of the document window.
To get a structure for the CAS number:
1. Go to Add-ins>ChemACX.com Structure from CAS Number.

2. Enter the CAS number.

3. Click Search. The corresponding structure is displayed in the document window.
Note: The CAS Registry Numbers provided in ChemACX.com have not been verified by CAS and may be inaccurate.
