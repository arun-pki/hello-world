Synthesis and Retrosynthesis

ChemDraw includes two tools to help you draw synthesis reactions. The Synthesis tool draws a synthesis reaction based on a product structure that you specify. Alternatively, the Retrosynthesis tool draws the reaction with the product on the left and an arrow pointing to the reactants. These tools are in the fragmentation tools palette of the main tools palette.
