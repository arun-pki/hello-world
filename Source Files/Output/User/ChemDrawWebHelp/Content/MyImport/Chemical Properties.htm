Chemical Properties

Chemical Property Prediction is a feature supported by ChemOffice. It enables you to calculate values for a wide range of methodologies; for example, topological descriptors such as the Weiner index and Balaban index, thermodynamic descriptors such as solubility, melting and boiling points, and partition coefficients such as CLogP, properties related to the three-dimensional shape and volume of a chemical such as the Connolly surface area and volume. In total, you can calculate more than 100 chemical properties for a given structure.
