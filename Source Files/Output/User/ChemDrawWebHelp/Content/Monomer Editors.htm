Monomer Editors

In ChemDraw 18.0 three types of Monomer Viewers or Editors are available:
Standard Monomer Viewer
Custom Monomer Editor
Imported Monomer Editor
You can access the monomer editors from the HELM Monomers toolbar. Depending on the type of data source (Standard, Custom or Imported) you selected, the respective viewer or editor is displayed when you click on the Open Monomer Editor button.
